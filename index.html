Privacy Policy for Expense Tracker

Last Updated: September 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Expense Tracker. This App is designed to help you track your personal expenses. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains that we do not collect or store your personal information on our own servers.

2. Information We Handle

  • Google User Information: If you choose to sign in with Google, we use Google's authentication service to verify your identity. We may access your basic profile information (name, email address, profile picture) to display within the app. This information is used solely for personalizing your in-app experience.
  • Financial Data: You provide financial data such as expenses, income, categories, and budget amounts. This data is stored directly in your own private Google Sheet, which you control. The App acts as an interface to read and write to your Google Sheet via Google's secure APIs. We do not have access to, nor do we store, a copy of your Google Sheet or the financial data within it on any servers we control.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core functionality of the App, which is to display, add, and analyze your financial data from your Google Sheet.
  • To authenticate your account and personalize your experience within the app.

4. Data Storage and Security

All your financial data is stored in your personal Google Drive, within a Google Sheet that you own and manage. We rely on the security measures provided by Google to protect your data. We recommend you review Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how they secure your data.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google's APIs (Google Sign-In and Google Sheets API) to function. Your use of these services is subject to Google's terms and privacy policies.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app or on this page.
